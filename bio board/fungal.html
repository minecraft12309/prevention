Fungal Diseases

Fungal diseases, also known as mycoses, are infections caused by fungi. Fungi can exist in various forms, including yeasts, molds, and mushrooms, and they can affect different parts of the body, including the skin, lungs, and bloodstream.

Ringworm

  • Causative Organism: Trichophyton species, Microsporum species, Epidermophyton species

  • Transmission: Direct contact with infected individuals or animals, indirect contact with contaminated surfaces, clothing, or personal items, and exposure to environments where the fungus thrives (e.g., swimming pools, locker rooms).

  • Symptoms: Itchy, red, circular patches with clearer skin in the center (ring appearance), scaling or flaky skin, and in cases of scalp infection (tinea capitis), bald patches and redness; nail infections (tinea unguium) may cause thickened, discolored nails.

  • Prevention: Good hygiene practices (regular handwashing), avoiding sharing personal items, wearing sandals in public showers, and keeping skin dry.

  • Treatment: Topical antifungal medications (e.g., clotrimazole, terbinafine) for skin infections; oral antifungal medications (e.g., griseofulvin) for more extensive infections like tinea capitis or nail infections.

Athlete's Foot

  • Causative Organism: Trichophyton rubrum, Trichophyton interdigitale, Epidermophyton floccosum

  • Transmission: Direct contact with infected skin or surfaces (e.g., locker rooms, swimming pools, shoes, and socks); can also spread from one part of the body to another.

  • Symptoms: Itching, burning, and stinging between the toes or on the soles of the feet; redness, peeling, and cracking skin; blisters may form, and in severe cases, the infection can spread to the toenails.

  • Prevention: Keeping feet clean and dry, wearing breathable footwear, using antifungal powder in shoes, avoiding walking barefoot in communal areas, and not sharing towels or footwear.

  • Treatment: Topical antifungal medications (e.g., clotrimazole, terbinafine, miconazole); oral antifungal medications may be needed for severe cases or nail involvement.
